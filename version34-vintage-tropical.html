The Terrace
The Space

Open sky, open arms.

Twelve stories above Brooklyn, our terrace is wrapped in market-string lights, terracotta tile, and the kind of breeze that makes you forget which borough you're in.

Hand-painted murals from local Boricua artists. Vintage Puerto Rican plena spinning on vinyl. Every surface tells a story.

The Pour

Liquid Caribbean.

Our bar program is anchored in Puerto Rican rum — aged, white, spiced, and the small-batch stuff our bartender smuggled back in his suitcase. Every cocktail is a postcard.

Non-drinkers get the same love. Our zero-proof menu is just as fearless.
