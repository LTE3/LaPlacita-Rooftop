Our Story

A sliver of San Juan tucked above the Brooklyn skyline. We pour island spirits, plate small bites kissed by sofrito, and chase the sun until the moon takes its turn.

The Space

Open sky, open arms.

Twelve stories above Brooklyn, our terrace is wrapped in market-string lights, terracotta tile, and the kind of breeze that makes you forget which borough you're in.

By day, brunch under the umbrellas. By night, the skyline becomes the wallpaper.

The Pour

Liquid Caribbean.

Our bar program is anchored in Puerto Rican rum — aged, white, spiced, and the small-batch stuff our bartender smuggled back in his suitcase.

Every cocktail is a postcard. Non-drinkers get the same love — our zero-proof menu is just as fearless.
