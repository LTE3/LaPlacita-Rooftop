OUR STORY

Born in
the heart of
Brooklyn.

La Placita is a love letter to Puerto Rico — twelve floors above the streets of Brooklyn, where the salt air still carries the sound of conga and the glow of neon paints the skyline pink.

From bartenders who treat rum like alchemy to DJs who refuse to let the dance floor cool, every night is a slow burn from sunset to last call.
