our story

Brooklyn's
warmest
twelfth floor.

La Placita is a love letter to Puerto Rico, served with a Brooklyn skyline. Pink-and-cyan sunsets, conga drums on the breeze, the slow burn of a perfect rum cocktail in your hand.

Every weekend our DJs spin from sunset to last call. Every weeknight we cool it down with salsa, tapas, and skyline views that make the city feel like an island.
