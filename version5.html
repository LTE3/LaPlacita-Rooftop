About
A Slice of Borinquen
Above Brooklyn.

— Tropical luxury, island roots.

La Placita Rooftop is a Puerto Rican bar perched above the streets of Brooklyn, where the warmth of the island meets the pulse of New York. Sip rum-forward cocktails under string lights, share small plates with friends, and let the breeze carry you somewhere a little further south.

The Terrace
— 01 / The Space The Rooftop

Open sky.
Open hearts.

Our terrace is built for golden hours and long nights — a wide, breezy space where palm-print umbrellas frame the Brooklyn skyline. Tropical greenery, weathered wood, and warm light make every seat feel like a corner of the Caribbean.

The Bar
— 02 / The Drinks The Cocktails

Rum, fruit,
and the sea.

Our cocktail program leans into Puerto Rican rum and tropical fruit — piña, passionfruit, guava, lime — built around classic island techniques. Every drink is poured to feel like sunset on the malecón, even when you're four stories above Brooklyn.
