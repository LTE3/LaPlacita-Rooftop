Our House

A piece of the island, perched above Brooklyn.

La Placita is a love letter to Puerto Rico — to lazy afternoons, sea-salted breezes, and abuela's kitchen. We built a rooftop where coquito meets coffee crema, where bomba drums find the skyline, and where every guest is family by the second sip.

Come for the cocktails. Stay for the warmth.

— Familia La Placita
The Bar

What awaits

Three reasons to come upstairs.

A rooftop built for long evenings — drinks worth a second round, plates worth sharing, and a view that does the work.

Cocktail craft

Island spirits, hand-pressed juice, and a bar team that treats every drink like a story worth telling — slow, sunlit, generous.

Latin plates

Mofongo, lechón sliders, tostones with garlic ali-oli — recipes pulled from family kitchens in San Juan and Loíza, plated for sharing.

Skyline sunsets

Twelve floors up, facing west — the Williamsburg Bridge in golden hour and Manhattan glowing pink behind your second drink.

The Terrace

Open-air, all summer long.

Linen umbrellas, swaying palms, and tables built for lingering. Reserve a corner sofa for sunset or grab a stool at the marble bar — the rooftop is yours.
