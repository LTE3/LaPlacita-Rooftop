The Details / 05

More than
a rooftop.

01

Loud by Design

DJs Thursday through Sunday. Salsa, perreo, dembow, and old-school boleros when the sun finally drops.

02

Open Sky

Retractable roof, exposed brick, hand-painted murals from local Boricua artists. Looks like a postcard, sounds like a block party.

03

Late Nights

Friday and Saturday we don't close until 2am. The kitchen runs late-night small plates after 11pm.

01

Island First

Our chef grew up in Loíza. The menu is built around that — pernil, mofongo, alcapurrias, tostones rellenos.

02

Small Plates

Built for sharing. Order three, four, six things. Pass them around. Nothing on the menu is precious.

03

Brunch Saturdays

Saturday brunch is loud. Mimosas with passion fruit, eggs over plantains, and the DJ starts at noon.

01

Find Us

Brooklyn, NY. Three flights up — the door is unmarked, just look for the painted flag on the brick.

02

Getting Here

L train, J train, walking distance from a few stops. We'll text you exact directions when you book.

03

Private Events

Buy out the rooftop for birthdays, weddings, or whatever. Capacity 120. Email events@laplacita.nyc.
