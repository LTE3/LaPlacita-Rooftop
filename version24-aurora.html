Our Story

Born of salt, brass, and Brooklyn dusk.

La Placita is a love letter. To San Juan summers, to the slow burn of aged rum, to the moment a salsa break splits the room in half. Twelve floors above the borough, we built a plaza where the sky does the talking and the band finishes the sentence.
