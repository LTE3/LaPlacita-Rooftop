The Plaza in the Sky

A night market on a Brooklyn rooftop.

We grew up watching abuelita haggle in San Juan's plaza markets — fish vendors hollering, frituras steaming, kids weaving through tables of mango and gandules. La Placita Rooftop is that energy, lifted six stories above Williamsburg.

Twelve stalls. One terrace. Endless string lights. Order from any window, find a table, and let the night unfold.

— The Terrace —

Six stories up, the night just gets started.

Our open-air terrace seats 180 around the bar, the kitchen, and a small stage. Wrap-around banquettes, low palm-leaf canopies, and string lights overhead make every table feel like a corner of the plaza.
