01 § 01 — The Place

A Corner Of
The Caribbean,
Five Stories Up.

Brooklyn · 40.6782 N

La Placita is more than a rooftop. It's a placita — a town square — lifted above Brooklyn and tuned to the rhythm of San Juan. Salsa on the speakers, ron in the glass, plantains crackling in the kitchen.

We build everything around three primary forms: warmth, soul, and sunset. Come for the view. Stay for the people.
