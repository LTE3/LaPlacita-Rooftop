Our Story

Brooklyn Meets the Island

Tropical cocktails poured under string lights, Latin-inspired plates served family-style, and Manhattan skyline sunsets that linger long after the sun goes down. La Placita brings the warmth of Puerto Rico to a Brooklyn rooftop — an island escape three stories above the street.

The Bar

Craft Cocktails

From smoky mezcal palomas to passion-fruit daiquiris and house-aged rum old fashioneds, every drink is built around bright island flavors and small-batch spirits. Sip something new — or rediscover a classic done right.
