Our Story

A sliver of San Juan tucked above the Brooklyn skyline. We pour island spirits, plate small bites kissed by sofrito, and chase the sun until the moon takes its turn. This is not a bar. It's a block party in the clouds.

The Terrace 12F
The Space

Open sky, open arms.

Twelve stories above Brooklyn, our terrace is wrapped in market-string lights, terracotta tile, and the kind of breeze that makes you forget which borough you're in. By day, brunch under the umbrellas. By night, the skyline becomes the wallpaper.

Hand-painted murals from local Boricua artists. Vintage Puerto Rican plena spinning on vinyl. Every surface tells a story.

The Pour

Liquid Caribbean.

Our bar program is anchored in Puerto Rican rum — aged, white, spiced, and the small-batch stuff our bartender smuggled back in his suitcase. Every cocktail is a postcard: a mango-pasion margarita, a smoked-coconut old fashioned, a coquito that drinks like dessert.

Non-drinkers get the same love. Our zero-proof menu is just as fearless.
