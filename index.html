Our Story

A Taste of
the Island, Above
the City

La Placita Rooftop brings the warmth and vibrance of Puerto Rico to the heart of Brooklyn. Set above the city skyline, we offer handcrafted tropical cocktails, authentic Latin cuisine, and an atmosphere that transports you to the plazas of Old San Juan.

Rooftop Views

Panoramic skyline views from our open-air terrace.

Craft Cocktails

Puerto Rican-inspired drinks with premium spirits.

Latin Kitchen

Authentic island flavors with a modern Brooklyn twist.

Live Music

Weekend DJ sets and live Latin performances.
