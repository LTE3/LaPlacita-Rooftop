— The Terrace, Golden Hour
Chapter One

A small piece of San Juan above the Brooklyn skyline.

La Placita is a love letter to the Puerto Rican plaza — a place where neighbors gather, music spills into the street, and the air smells of salt and citrus.

Six stories up, our terrace catches the East River breeze and the last light of the day. Stay for one drink. Stay for all of them.
